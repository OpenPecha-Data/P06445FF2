dharmaśīlān śūcīn dakṣān kāryajñān śāstrakovidān|kṛtavṛttīn samān snigdhān vṛddhānadhikṛtān
kuru

pratimāsaṃ ca tebhyastvaṃ sarvamāyavyayaṃ śṛṇu|śrutvā dharmādhikārādyaṃ kāryaṃ sarvaṃ svayaṃ
vada

dharmārthaṃ yadi te rājyaṃ na kīrtyarthaṃ na kāmataḥ|tataḥ saphalamatyarthamanarthārthamato'nyathā

parasparāmiṣībhūte loko'smin prāyaśo nṛpa|yathā rājyaṃ ca dharmaśca bhavettava tathā śṛṇu

jñānavṛddhāḥ kule jātā nyāyajñāḥ pāpabhīravaḥ|sametā bahavo nityaṃ santu te kāryadarśinaḥ

daṇḍabandhaprahāradīn kuryuste nyāyato'pi cet|kāruṇyārdraḥ sadā bhūtvā tvamanugrahavān bhava

hitāyaiva tvayā cittamunnāmyaṃ sarvadehinām|kāruṇyātsatataṃ rājaṃstīvrapāpakṛtāmapi

tīvrapāpeṣu hiṃsreṣu kṛpā kāryā viśeṣataḥ|ta eva hi kṛpāsthānaṃ hatātmāno mahātmanām

pratyahaṃ pañcarātraṃ vā baddhān kṣīṇān vimocaya|śeṣānapi yathāyogaṃ mā kāṃścit naiva mocaya

yeṣvamokṣaṇacittaṃ te jāyate teṣvasaṃvaraḥ|tasmādasaṃvarāt pāpamajasramupacīyate

yāvacca na vimucyeraṃstāvatsyuḥ sukhabandhanāḥ|nāpitasnānapānānnabhaiṣajyavasanānvitāḥ

apātreṣviva putreṣu pātrīkaraṇakāṅkṣayā|kāruṇyā[ttāḍanaṃ kāryaṃ na dveṣān]nārthalipsayā

vimṛśya samyagvijñāya praduṣṭān ghātakānapi|ahatvā pīḍayitvā ca kuru nirviṣayān narān

svatantraḥ paśya sarvaṃ ca viṣayaṃ cāracakṣuṣā|nityāpramattaḥ smṛtimān kuru kāryaṃ ca dhārmikam

pradānamānasatkārairguṇasthān satataṃ bhaja|udārairanurūpaistu śeṣānapi yathāvidhi

saṃmānasphītakusumaḥ saṃpradānamahāphalaḥ|rājavṛkṣaḥ kṣamācchāyaḥ sevyate bhṛtyapakṣibhiḥ

tyāgaśīlamayo rājā tejasvī bhavati priyaḥ|śarkarāmodako yadvadelāmaricakarkaśaḥ

mātsyanyāyaśca te naivaṃ nyāyādrājyaṃ bhaviṣyati|na cānyāyo na vādharmo dharmaścaivaṃ bhaviṣyati

paralokāttvayā rājyaṃ nānītaṃ nāpi neṣyasi|dharmāt prāptamato'syārthe nādharmaṃ kartumarhasi

rājyena bhāṇḍamūlyena duḥkhabhāṇḍaparaṃparām|rājan yathā nārjayasi prayatnaḥ kriyatāṃ tathā

rājyena bhāṇḍamūlyena rājyabhāṇḍaparaṃparām|rājan yathā nirviśasi prayatnaḥ kriyatāṃ tathā

caturdvīpamapi prāpya pṛthivīṃ cakravartinaḥ|śārīraṃ mānasaṃ caiva sukhadvayamidaṃ matam

duḥkhapratikriyāmātraṃ śārīraṃ vedanāsukham|saṃjñāmayaṃ mānasaṃ tu kevalaṃ kalpanākṛtam

duḥkhapratikriyāmātraṃ kalpanāmātrameva ca|lokasya sukhasarvasvaṃ vyarthametadato'rthataḥ

dvīpadeśapurāvāsapradeśasthānavāsasām|śayyānnapānahastyaśvastrīṇāṃ caikaikabhogyatā

yadā ca yatra cittaṃ syāt tadānena sukhaṃ kila|śeṣāṇāmamanaskārātteṣāṃ vyarthatvamarthataḥ

viṣayān pañcabhiḥ pañca cakṣurādibhirindriyaiḥ|na kalpayati cedgaṇhan nāsmātteṣu tadā sukham

jānīte viṣayaṃ yaṃ yaṃ yena yenendriyeṇa ca|tadā na śeṣaiḥ śeṣāṇi vyarthānyeva yatastadā

indriyairupalabdhasya viṣayasyākṛtiṃ manaḥ|upalabhya vyatītasya kalpayan manyate sukham

ekamarthaṃ vijānāti yadyapyekamihendriyam|tadapyarthaṃ vinā vyarthaṃ vyartho'rtho'pi ca tadvinā

pratītya mātāpitarau yathoktaḥ putrasaṃbhavaḥ|cakṣūrūpe pratītyaivamukto vijñānasaṃbhavaḥ

atītānāgatā vyarthā viṣayāḥ sārdhamindriyaiḥ|tadudvayānatiriktatvād vyarthā ye'pi ca sāṃpratāḥ

alātacakraṃ gṛṇhāti yathā cakṣurviparyayāt|tathendriyāṇi gṛṇhanti viṣayān sāṃpratāniva

indriyāṇindriyāerthāśca pañcabhūtamayā matāḥ|pratisvaṃ bhūtavaiyarthyādeṣāṃ vyarthatvamarthataḥ

nirindhano'gnirbhūtānāṃ vinirbhāge prasajyate|saṃparke lakṣaṇābhāvaḥ śeṣeṣvapyeṣa nirṇayaḥ

evaṃ dvidhāpi bhūtānāṃ vyarthatvātsaṃgatirvṛthā|vyarthatvātsaṃgateścaivaṃ rūpaṃ vyarthamato'rthataḥ

vijñānavedanāsaṃjñāsaṃskārāṇāṃ ca sarvaśaḥ|pratyekamātmavaiyarthyadvaiyarthyaṃ paramārthataḥ

sukhābhimāno duḥkhasya pratīkāre yathārthataḥ|tathā sukhābhimāno'pi sukhasya pratighātajaḥ

sukhe saṃyogatṛṣṇaivaṃ naiḥsvābhāvyātprahīyate|duḥkhe viyogatṛṣṇā ca paśyatāṃ muktirityataḥ

kaḥ paśyatīti ceccittaṃ vyahāreṇa kathyate|na hi caittaṃ vinā cittaṃ vyarthatvānna saheṣyate

vyarthamevaṃ jaganmatvā yāthābhūtyānnirāspadaḥ|nirvāti nirupādāno nirupādānavanhivat

bodhisattvo'pi dṛṣṭaivaṃ saṃbodhau niyato mataḥ|kevalaṃ tasya kāruṇyādā bodherbhavasaṃtatiḥ

bodhisattvasya saṃbhāro mahāyāne tathāgataiḥ|nirdiṣṭaḥ sa tu saṃmūḍhaiḥ pradviṣṭaścaiva nindyate

guṇadoṣānabhijño vā doṣasaṃjñī guṇeṣu vā|athavāpi guṇadveṣī mahāyānasya nindakaḥ

paropaghātino doṣān parānugrāhiṇo guṇān|jñātvocyate guṇadveṣī mahāyānasya nindakaḥ

yatsvārthanirapekṣatvāt parārthaikarasapriyam|guṇākaraṃ mahāyānaṃ taddviṣī tena dahyate

śrāddho'pi durgṛhītena dviṣyāt kruddho'thavetaraḥ|śrāddho'pi dagdha ityuktaḥ kā cintā dveṣābandhure

viṣeṇāpi viṣaṃ hanyādyathaivoktaṃ cikitsakaiḥ|duḥkhenāpyahitaṃ hanyādityukte kiṃ virudhyate

manaḥpūrvaṃgamā dharmā manaḥśreṣṭhā iti śruteḥ|hitaṃ hitamanāḥ kurvan duḥkhenāpyahitaṃ katham

duḥkhamapyāyatīpathyaṃ kāryaṃ kimu sukhaṃ hitam|ātmanaśca pareṣāṃ ca dharma eṣā sanātanaḥ

mātrāsukhaparityāgāt paścāccedvipulaṃ sukham|tyajenmātrāsukhaṃ dhīraḥ saṃpaśyan vipulaṃ sukham

na mṛśyate ca yadyetat kaṭubhaiṣajyadāyinaḥ|tataścikitsakādyāśca hatā naivaṃ ca yujyate

apathyamapi yaddṛṣṭaṃ tatpathyaṃ paṇḍitaiḥ kvacit|utsargaścāpavādaśca sarvaśāstreṣu śasyate

karuṇāpūrvakāḥ sarve niṣyandā jñānanirmalāḥ|uktā yatra mahāyāne kastannindetsacetanaḥ

atyaudāryātigāmbhīryādviṣaṇṇairakṛtātmabhiḥ|nindyate'dya mahāyānaṃ mohāt svaparavairibhiḥ

dānaśīlakṣamāvīryadhyānaprajñākṛpātmakam|mahāyānamatastasmin kasmāddurbhāṣitaṃ vacaḥ

parārtho dānaśīlābhyāṃ kṣāntyā vīryeṇa cātmanaḥ|dhyānaṃ prajñā ca mokṣāya mahāyānārthasaṃgrahaḥ

parā[tmahita]mokṣārthāḥ saṃkṣepādbuddhaśāsanam|te ṣaṭpāramitāgarbhāstasmād bauddhamidaṃ vacaḥ

puṇyajñānamayo yatra buddhairbodhermahāpathaḥ|deśitastanmahāyānamajñānādvai na dṛśyate

khamivācintyaguṇatvādukto'cintyaguṇo jinaḥ|mahāyāne yato buddhamāhātmyaṃ kṣamyatāmidam

āryaśāradvatasyāpi śīlamātre'pyagocaraḥ|yasmāt tadbuddhamāhātmyamacintyaṃ kiṃ na mṛṣyate

anutpādo mahāyāne paresāṃ śūnyatā kṣayaḥ|kṣayānutpādayośrcaikyamarthataḥ kṣamyatāṃ yataḥ

śūnyatā buddhamāhātmyamevaṃ yuktyānupaśyatām|mahāyānetaroktāni na sameyuḥ kathaṃ satām

tathāgatābhisaṃdhyoktānyasukhaṃ jñātumityataḥ|ekayānatriyānoktādātmā rakṣya upekṣayā

upekṣayā hi nāpuṇyaṃ dvesātpāpaṃ kutaḥ śubham|mahāyāne yato dveṣo nātmakāmaiḥ kṛto'rhati

na bodhisattvapraṇidhirna caryāpariṇāmanā|uktāḥ śrāvakayāne'smādbodhisattvaḥ kutastataḥ

adhiṣṭhānāni noktāni bodhisattvasya bodhaye|buddhairanyatpramāṇaṃ ca ko'sminnarthe jinādhikaḥ

adhiṣṭhānāryasatyārthabodhipakṣopasaṃhitāt|mārgācchāvakasāmānyādbauddhaṃ kenādhikaṃ phalam

bodhicaryāpratiṣṭhārtha na sūtre bhāṣitaṃ vacaḥ|bhāṣitaṃ ca mahāyāne grāhyamasmādvicakṣaṇaiḥ

yathaiva vaiyākaraṇo mātṛkāmapi pāṭhayet|buddho'vadattathā dharmaṃ vineyānāṃ yathākṣamam

keṣāṃcidavadaddharmaṃ pāpebhyo vinivṛttaye|keṣāṃcitpuṇyasiddhayarthaṃ keṣāṃcidū dvayaniśritam

dvayāniśritamekeṣāṃ gambhīraṃ bhīrubhīṣaṇam|śūnyatākaruṇāgarbhamekeṣāṃ bodhisādhanam

iti sadbhirmahāyāne kartavyaḥ pratighakṣayaḥ|prasādaścādhikaḥ kāryaḥ samyaksaṃbodhisiddhaye

mahāyānaprasādena taduktācaraṇena ca|prāpyate'nuttarā bodhiḥ sarvasaukhyāni cāntarā

dānaṃ śīlaṃ kṣamā satyaṃ gṛhasthasya viśeṣataḥ|dharma uktaḥ kṛpāgarbhaḥ sa sātmīkriyatāṃ dṛḍham

atha lokasya vaidharmyādrājyaṃ dharmeṇa duṣkaram|tato dharmayaśorthaṃ te pravrajyādhigamaḥ kṣamaḥ

ratnāvatyāṃ rājavṛttopadeśo nāma caturthaḥ paricchedaḥ||
